ABOUT US

A Community Based Organization in Kisii County Started in August 2022; with 3 interests:
1. Implementing Health Programs,
2. Conduct Research and Disseminate information on key programmatic areas
3. Train and Capacity build health care workers in key programmatic areas

STRATEGIC PILLARS


HEALTH:

In alignment with our commitment to community well-being, our pillar of "Health" stands as a cornerstone of HECOWENA.
We strive to enhance and promote the overall health of communities we serve.
This involves implementing comprehensive health programs, fostering preventive care, and addressing the diverse healthcare needs of individuals.
Through innovative and accessible health initiatives, we aim to build resilient and thriving communities.

RESEARCH:

At HECOWENA, the "Research" pillar underscores our dedication to continuous learning and advancement.
We actively engage in research endeavors to understand the unique challenges and opportunities within the communities we serve.
This commitment allows us to develop evidence-based strategies, implement impactful interventions, and stay at the forefront of emerging trends.
Through rigorous research, we seek to contribute valuable insights that drive positive change and development.

TRAINING:

Empowering individuals and communities through knowledge and skill development is the essence of our "Training" pillar.
HECOWENA is devoted to providing high-quality training programs that equip individuals with the tools and expertise needed for personal and professional growth.
From vocational training to capacity-building initiatives, we believe that investing in education and skill development is fundamental to unlocking the potential of communities.
Our training efforts aim to create a sustainable impact, fostering self-reliance and contributing to the overall prosperity of the nation.
