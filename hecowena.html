ABOUT US

A Community Based Organization in Kisii County Started in August 2022; with 3 interests:
1. Implementing Health Programs,
2. Conduct Research and Disseminate information on key programmatic areas
3. Train and Capacity build health care workers in key programmatic areas

STRATEGIC PILLARS


HEALTH:

HECOWENA focuses on community well-being via the "Health" pillar, improving overall health through comprehensive programs and innovative initiatives for resilient, thriving communities.

RESEARCH:

our commitment to continuous learning. We actively engage in research to understand community challenges, develop evidence-based strategies, and stay ahead of emerging trends. Our goal is to contribute valuable insights for positive change and development.

TRAINING

HECOWENA's "Training" pillar empowers individuals and communities with quality programs, fostering personal and professional growth through vocational and capacity-building initiatives. The commitment to education and skill development aims to unlock community potential, promote self-reliance, and contribute to national prosperity.
